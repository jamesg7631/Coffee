Calculating total cost of a car rental trip

Hey there! This problem might be a bit unpredictable, but give it a go and let us know how you do!
A company providing car rental services has a fixed pricing plan for every kilometer traveled. This price is a constant and will not change. Create a program that takes as input (in a single line) the number of kilometers a customer has traveled and outputs the total cost of the trip. Assume that the fixed cost of travel per kilometer is 10 units.
Sample Input:
20

Sample Output:
200


Sample Input:
30

Sample Output:
300



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 5 seconds
